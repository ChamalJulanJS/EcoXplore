Our Ethos

Pioneering the intersection of ultra-luxury travel and radical ecological conservation in Sri Lanka.

Born from the
Wilderness.

EcoXplore was forged by a collective of Sri Lankan environmentalists who saw the devastating impact of mass tourism. We set out to create an alternative: experiences that are breathtakingly beautiful, exceptionally luxurious, and entirely restorative to the ecosystems they inhabit.

Every journey booked with us directly funds reforestation in the Sinharaja rainforest and supports marginalized indigenous communities across the central highlands.
